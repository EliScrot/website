Shelly

Shelly is a Common Starting Brawler that is unlocked upon beginning the game. She has moderate health and damage output. Her shotgun deals more damage the closer she is to her target, making her excellent for short-range combat. Her attacks have a wide spread as well.

El Primo

El Primo is a Rare Brawler that has the second-highest health of any Brawler, a high damage output, and great mobility with his Super and his fast movement speed, but a very short attack range. El Primo's Trait allows him to charge his Super from enemies damaging him.
